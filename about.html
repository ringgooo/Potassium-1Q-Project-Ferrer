ABOUT

I am a student from the Philippine Science High School SOCSKSARGEN Region Campus and I chose this topic to raise awareness about the struggles faced by Filipino farmers, who are the backbone of our nation's agricultural sector. By spreading awareness, I hope to inspire more people, leaders, and communities alike to recognize the struggles of our farmers and take part in building a future where agriculture is not only productive, but also progresive, fair, and sustainable for those who dedicate their blood, sweat, tears, and lives on it.
